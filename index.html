Process

How I design a learning solution with ADDIE

  1. A

    Analyse

    Clarify learners, context, performance gaps, constraints, and success criteria.

  2. D

    Design

    Map learning objectives, assessment strategy, content flow, and experience structure.

  3. D

    Develop

    Create materials, interactions, media, activities, and review-ready learning assets.

  4. I

    Implement

    Launch the learning experience, support facilitators and learners, and monitor delivery.

  5. E

    Evaluate

    Measure effectiveness, collect feedback, and refine the solution through evidence.
